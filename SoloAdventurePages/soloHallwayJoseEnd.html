Go Investigate the footsteps!

Jacob decides to walk in the well-lit path to see what is making those footstep sounds. As he heads forward, Jacob notices a shadowy figure running down the zig zagging hallway so he reacts by chasing down whoever is running. Jacob is lead into an end of a hallway with a door slowly closing down to cover the room's entrance. Without thinking, Jacob slides under the door before the room is completely sealed and sees Jose and a large computer set up. Jacob ask why does he have so many computers running here in this dungeon and Jose explains that he is mining Cryptos and needs a secret spot to mine them. Jose then bribes Jacob to keep this location a secret from anyone especially from a person named Kennifer who Jose himself is terrified of and gives Jacob compensation via bitcoin.

Jacob is rich from this discovery and Jose's secret crypto mining operation is safe from anyone. Good Ending!
